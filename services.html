Services

Whether you’re looking to get your closet organized or earn extra cash from clothes you no longer wear, I offer personalized services designed just for you.

Closet Cleanouts

Closet Cleanout Service Ready for a wardrobe refresh? I come to your home and help you sort, clean, and organize your closet—no stress, no mess. We won’t just toss clothes you no longer love; we’ll give them a second life with someone who will. You can book me for an hourly fee, or I’ll resell the pieces you part with and pay you a percentage of the earnings. Let’s turn clutter into confidence and make room for your next favorite outfit.

Closet Organization

Let’s sort, style, and simplify your space. I’ll work with you to declutter your closet, organize everything by category or color, and create a setup that fits your lifestyle. Whether you need a quick refresh or a full closet overhaul, I’ll guide you through the process with hands-on support. You’ll also receive personalized tips and product recommendations to help you maintain your beautifully organized space long after I leave. Together, we’ll transform your closet into a space that feels calm, functional, and uniquely you.

Resale Service

I handle the entire resale process from start to finish—so you don’t have to lift a finger. From taking high-quality photos and writing eye-catching listings to setting fair prices and managing all the shipping and communication, I take care of it all. You’ll earn 40% of the final sale price, and I’ll retain 60% for managing the time, effort, and logistics involved. It’s a hassle-free way to give your clothes a second life while earning money back on pieces you no longer wear.
