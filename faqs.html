Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the Frequently Asked Questions regarding my services.

1 Do I need to be home during the organizing session?

Yes, for both safety and liability reasons, your presence is required throughout the entire organization session. Having you home not only ensures that your belongings are handled with care and transparency, but also allows for real-time collaboration—so I can ask questions, get your input, and make sure every decision reflects your preferences. Your involvement helps make the process smooth, personalized, and efficient.

2 What happens to the clothes that don’t sell?

If items don’t sell within a certain time frame, you have options! I can return them to you, donate them to a local charity, or include them in a future sale. It’s totally up to you.

3 How long does a typical closet cleanout take? ?

It depends on the size and condition of your closet, but most sessions range from 2 to 5 hours. We’ll discuss the scope beforehand to give you a better time estimate.

1 How does the resale service work?

I take care of the full process: photography, listings, pricing, shipping, and customer communication. You earn 40% of each sale, and I keep 60% for managing the work.

2 What items can you resell?

I focus on gently worn women’s clothing, shoes, and accessories. High-demand brands or quality basics sell best.

3 When do I get paid for sold items?

Payouts are made monthly for any items sold during that period. You’ll receive a summary of what sold and your earnings.

1 What happens to items that don’t sell?

After a set time (typically 60–90 days), unsold items can be returned to you, donated, or marked down—your choice.

2 Where are you located and what areas do you serve?

I’m based in Nashville, and I offer in-home services within [Service Area]. Virtual consultations are also available.

3 What should I do to prepare before the session?

All that I ask is that your closet is accessible and any harmful objects are placed elsewhere during the process. I’ll guide you through the process step by step.
